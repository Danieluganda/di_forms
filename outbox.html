5. Your rights

You are under no obligation to give your consent. You are eligible to participate in all aspects of the Program, regardless of your decision to share your data. Consent to data sharing is entirely separate from Program participation. You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: You have the right to request a copy of the information we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You have the right to ask us to correct any inaccurate information we hold about you.
  • Right to erasure: You have the right to ask us to delete your information.
  • Right to restriction of processing: You have the right to ask us to restrict the processing of your information.
  • Right to object to processing: You have the right to object to the processing of your information.
  • Right to data portability: You have the right to request that your data be transferred to another organisation.
  • Right to withdraw consent: You have the right to withdraw your consent to share your data at any time. This will not affect your ability to participate in or continue participating in the Program.
